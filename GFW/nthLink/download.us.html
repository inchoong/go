nthLink is an application that allows you to browse the internet freely without website or content blocking restrictions. The application is simple to set up, and uses a mix of encryption and obfuscation technology to grant you safe access to the internet. nthLink undergoes regular security audits by an independent auditor, and operates under a no-logs policy, meaning they do not know who you are or which websites you visit while you’re using the application. What’s more, nthLink is committed to transparency in technology, and as such makes their codebase open source and available for the benefit of the internet freedom community's use.
